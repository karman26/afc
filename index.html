about us

American Freight Corporation specialize in hauling produce from the West coast to the Mid West and Texas. We anticipate building a legacy of excellence.

American Freight Corporation is a family business founded in 2005 that started out with 2 trucks. After years of hard work and dedication We have built this company up to 35 power units and 40 refrigerated trailers.
