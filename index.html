About Us

Darshan Dhaliwal founded American Freight Corporation with her husband in 2005 starting out with 2 trucks. After years of hard work and dedication they built the company up to 35 power units and 40 refrigerated trailers. American Freight Corporation specialize in hauling produce from the West coast to the Mid West and Texas. We anticipate building a legacy of excellence.
